Summary

QuizKids is an educational quiz application designed for children. The app works offline and does not require user registration. However, it displays advertisements using Google AdMob, which may collect certain technical identifiers to provide advertising services.

1. About QuizKids

QuizKids is an educational mobile application that provides interactive quizzes and drag-and-drop learning activities for children. Most of the app’s features function without an Internet connection. Internet access may be required to load advertisements.

2. Information We Collect

Information collected by the app

QuizKids does not require users to create an account and does not directly collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location data
  • User-generated personal data

Information collected by third-party services (Google AdMob)

QuizKids uses Google AdMob to display advertisements, including app open ads, banner ads, and interstitial ads. Google AdMob may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Advertising ID (Android Advertising Identifier)
  • Device identifiers such as App Set ID
  • Device information (device model, operating system version)
  • IP address (for ad delivery and fraud prevention)
  • Ad interaction data (ad views, clicks)

This information is collected and processed by Google according to their Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. How Information Is Used

The information collected by Google AdMob is used to:

  • Display advertisements within the application
  • Measure ad performance
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Improve advertising services

4. Offline Usage

QuizKids can function without an Internet connection for educational content. However, advertisements may require an Internet connection to load and display.

5. Children’s Privacy

QuizKids is designed for educational purposes and may be used by children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

Advertisements are served through Google AdMob, which complies with applicable policies and regulations, including requirements for apps directed toward children when properly configured.

6. Third-Party Services

This application uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob — Advertising service
    Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

7. Data Security

QuizKids does not store personal user data on its own servers. Any data collected by third-party services is handled according to their own security policies.

8. User Rights

Users can manage advertising preferences through their device settings, including resetting or disabling the Advertising ID.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date on this page.
